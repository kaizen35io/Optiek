About Optiek Marisa

For over 40 years, we've been dedicated to providing exceptional optical care and premium eyewear across Suriname. Our commitment to quality, precision, and personal service sets us apart.

Since 1980 — Trusted optical care in Suriname

Our Story

Founded in 1980, Optiek Marisa has grown from a single optical shop to a trusted name in vision care across Suriname. With four locations serving communities from Paramaribo to Wanica, we combine decades of expertise with a modern approach to eye care.

We believe that everyone deserves to see the world clearly and look good doing it. That's why we curate the finest eyewear collections and provide personalized service that goes beyond expectations.
